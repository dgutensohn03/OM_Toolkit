O&M is a $9B, global organization with 14,000 teammates. Our mission is to empower our customers to advance healthcare. Since 1882, O&M has a tradition of providing sustainable supply chain, product, and patient solutions that lower total costs and improve quality across the continuum of care.

We have been, and will continue to be, committed to following all federal laws and global regulations and working with partners who share this same commitment.

Ethics and Compliance at O&M

O&M’s comprehensive Ethics and Compliance program aligns with fundamental elements of an effective compliance program, as outlined in the United States Department of Justice Evaluation of Corporate Compliance Principles Guidance Document, United States healthcare laws, and compliance industry best practices. This includes a well-designed compliance program that is implemented effectively and applied practically.

Elements of O&M’s compliance program include annual risk assessment, our Code of Honor, compliance policies and procedures, training and communications, confidential reporting and investigations, and third-party management. In addition, our compliance program provides compliance guidance and support of senior and middle management, privacy (including HIPAA and GDPR compliance), auditing and monitoring, expense management, and analysis and remediation of misconduct, including disciplinary measures.

Elements of O&M’s compliance program include, but are not limited to:
  • Annual risk assessment
  • Our Code of Honor
  • Compliance policies and procedures
  • Training and communications
  • Confidential reporting and investigations
  • Third-party management
  • Compliance guidance and support of senior and middle management
  • Privacy guidance (including HIPAA and GDPR compliance)
  • Transparency guidance
  • Auditing and monitoring
  • Expense management
  • Analysis and remediation of misconduct, including disciplinary measures

O&M Code of Honor

Our Company Code of Honor provides standards and guidelines for ethical conduct for all O&M teammates, officers, and directors. It outlines expected conduct, key compliance laws, workplace conduct, conflicts of interest guidelines, conducting business with our customers and suppliers, and integrity of our business and accounting practices.

All O&M associates are expected to comply with the Code of Honor at all times and immediately report any potential or actual violation of the Code of Honor. Anyone may view the Code of Honor and report a concern in any language at www.omicodeofhonor.com.

O&M IDEAL Values

O&M values reflect our commitment to our customers, our teammates, and the communities where we live and work. They embody “IDEAL” behavior—Integrity, Development, Excellence, Accountability, and Listening. We live our IDEAL values in everything we do, including our anti-corruption program. Learn more about IDEAL values in the O&M Code of Honor.
