Distributor Toolkit

At Owens & Minor, we value our trusted partners and view your business as critical to helping service patients around the world. As a Distributor Partner of our proprietary product brands, Halyard, MediChoice, and Medical Action, we expect your commitment to conducting business with integrity and compliance with all global, regional, and local laws. You are expected to create, implement, and maintain a comprehensive compliance program and avoid all forms of business corruption.

Toolkit
This Distributor Toolkit was created to help guide your organization toward compliant behavior with every employee, supplier, healthcare professional, and government official you work with. It contains useful compliance tools, training and resources for use. Please contact us anytime with any questions or concerns.
Some materials used within this Toolkit are available in English only. Please reach out for any necessary clarifications.

About Owens & Minor:

Headquartered in Richmond, Virginia, we are a Fortune 500 company operating our Global Solutions and Global Products segments, with core offerings structured across three pillars of excellence: Products, Services, and Distribution. Owens & Minor’s Global Products pillar offers an industry-leading portfolio of proprietary, self-manufactured and clinically-superior infection prevention, surgical solutions, and personal protective equipment (PPE) products under our HALYARD® brand as well as products, custom procedure trays and kits offered through our proprietary MediChoice® and Medical Action® brands.
